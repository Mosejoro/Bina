Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between BINA and BINACBT?

BINA is a platform that allows students to practice CBT exams on their phone, tablet, or computer anytime. It helps them prepare for exams like Common Entrance, JSCE, WAEC, and JAMB.

BINACBT is designed for schools. It allows schools to run their own computer-based exams, mock tests, and assessments for their students.

Can students use BINA on their phones?

Yes. Students can use BINA on phones, tablets, laptops, or desktop computers. This allows them to practice CBT exams anytime and become comfortable with the computer-based exam format.

Who can use BINA?

BINA is designed for students preparing for exams such as Common Entrance, JSCE, WAEC, and JAMB. It can also be used by teachers, lesson centres, and tutorial centres that want their students to practice CBT exams regularly.

What can schools do with BINACBT?

Schools can use BINACBT to conduct internal exams, mock exams, and practice CBT tests. The system allows schools to manage exams easily, monitor student performance, and get instant results.

How can a school get BINACBT?

Schools can request a BINACBT setup through the contact section of this website. Our team will help set up a customized CBT platform for the school and guide administrators on how to use it.
